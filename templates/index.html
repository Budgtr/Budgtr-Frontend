Budgtr?

Budgtr is designed to help you manage your money better. With this app, you can easily create a budget by organizing your allowance and any other money you get into different categories, like savings, snacks, and games. You can set limits for how much you want to spend in each category and get alerts if you’re close to overspending. This way, you can make sure you don’t run out of money too quickly and learn to spend wisely.

The app also helps you keep track of all your expenses. You can manually enter each purchase you make, so you always know where your money goes. This makes it easy to see your spending habits and find ways to save more. Plus, the app offers fun graphs and reports to show your spending in a simple, visual way. There are also tips and tutorials to help you learn more about saving money and reaching your financial goals, making money management both easy and fun!
